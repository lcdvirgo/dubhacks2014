DubHacks 2014 was a success!

With over 380 participants, 76 projects and 5000 pounds of Costco, DubHacks 2014 was the very first major collegiate hackathon in the Pacific Northwest, hosted at the University of Washington, Seattle on Oct 17-18.
Thanks for joining us and made history.
